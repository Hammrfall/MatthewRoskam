about me

I am a seasoned programmer with over eight years of experience. I have a B.A. from Samford University, and a full stack web developer certificate from the University of Central Florida. My work programming robotic systems controls gave me extensive experience in designing, developing, testing and deploying complex systems. My projects included connecting into a wide-range of customer databases (mySQL, Microsoft SQL, Oracle, Access, Excel and CSVs), and also providing intuitive interfaces for the users that provided real-time reporting of system status.


My work in web development has allowed me to work with the very latest frameworks and libraries as a true full-stack developer with experience in managing programmers, leading in architectural design, and programming in multiple languages on both the front and back ends. I also have experience in devOps, and server management as well as extensive experience in git, Agile development processes, and version control.


I am focused on providing elegant software solutions leveraging object-oriented programming techniques to provide maximum flexibility and code reusability. I also have a passion for providing simple, intuitive interfaces for the user experience. I have successfully led teams of programmers in the creative process of design and development, and in debugging and implementation. I enjoy the collaborative nature of well executed projects, and I am constantly looking for “+1” opportunities to exceed expectations.
