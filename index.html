About me

I am a seasoned programmer with over six years of experience. I have a B.A. from Samford University, and a full stack web developer certificate from the University of Central Florida. My work programming robotic systems controls gave me extensive experience in designing, developing, testing and deploying complex systems. My projects included connecting into a wide-range of customer databases (mySQL, Microsoft SQL, Oracle, Access, Excel and CSVs), and also providing intuitive interfaces for the users that provided real-time reporting of system status.


I am focused on providing elegant solutions by writing object based programs that provide maximum flexibility and code reusability. I also have a passion for providing simple, intuitive interfaces for the user experience. I have successfully led teams of programmers in the creative process of design and development, and in debugging and implementation. I enjoy the collaborative nature of well executed projects, and I am always looking for “+1” opportunities to exceed expectations.
